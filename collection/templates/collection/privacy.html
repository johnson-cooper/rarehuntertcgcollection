Privacy Policy

Rare Hunter TCG values your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a customer.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information you provide when placing orders (name, email, shipping address, phone number).
  • Payment information is handled securely by Stripe; Rare Hunter TCG does not store full credit card numbers.
  • Browser and usage data collected via cookies and analytics for site performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To communicate order updates and customer support.
  • To improve our website and products.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your information. Personal data may be shared with service providers (like Stripe and shipping carriers) solely for fulfilling orders.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and analytics to improve your browsing experience and track product popularity. You may disable cookies in your browser, but some functionality may be affected.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no website is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us via Instagram or email for any privacy-related requests.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version is always available on our website. Continued use of the site indicates acceptance of any changes.

8. Contact Us

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data can be directed to Instagram.
